Accreditation & Certification

Karma Lab is ISO certified for calibration and validation activities, ensuring that all services are performed under a structured quality management system. Our operations follow controlled procedures, traceable measurement standards, and documented validation protocols to meet regulatory and compliance requirements. This ensures consistent, accurate, and verifiable results for critical instruments, systems, and facilities.
