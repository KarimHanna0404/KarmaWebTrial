Cleanroom Services

With extensive experience in cleanroom validation and qualification, Karma Lab delivers reliable, compliant solutions across regulated environments. Our team combines technical expertise with industry best practices to support pharmaceutical, healthcare, and controlled facilities throughout every stage of validation.

Thermal Study & Validation
Temperature mapping • Stability checks

Thermal mapping and validation for areas/equipment such as storage, incubators, cold rooms, and controlled environments, with documented results suitable for QA review.

Machine Qualification
IQ • OQ • PQ

Qualification protocols and execution (IQ/OQ/PQ) for process and utility equipment, including documentation, verification tests, and report packs.

Medical Gases Qualification
Pipelines • Points of use

Qualification of medical gas systems (distribution, outlets, alarms where applicable), ensuring performance and compliance against relevant requirements.

BMS Validation
Monitoring • Alarms • Trending

Validation support for Building Management Systems: sensor verification, alarm checks, data integrity review, and documentation aligned with site SOPs.

GMP Consultation
Compliance • GAP assessment

Practical GMP guidance for cleanroom operations: gap assessments, documentation structure, SOP improvements, and readiness support for audits/inspections.

Decontamination
Surface • Room • Equipment

Decontamination service planning and execution support for controlled areas and equipment, with documented steps and verification as required.
